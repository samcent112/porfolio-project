DELL 12.3" Latitude 5290 Multi-Touch 2-in-1 Laptop PC Windows 10 Pro (No Keyboard)
With its 2-in-1 detachable design, the 12.3" Latitude 5290 Multi-Touch 2-in-1 Laptop from Dell is built for mobility and delivers the power of a laptop with the flexibility of a tablet. The Latitude 5290 2-in-1 measures just 0.42" thin and weighs 1.89 pounds. Please note that this configuration only includes the tablet and the Latitude 5290 2-in-1 Travel Keyboard is sold separately. However, an integrated auto-deploy kickstand helps to deliver a smooth user experience. Place your Latitude 5290 2-in-1 on a flat surface and the kickstand will automatically extend. It can rotate up to 150° for multiple viewing angles. The system's 12.3" Anti-Glare display is driven by integrated Intel UHD Graphics 620 and features a Full HD 1920 x 1080 resolution, a brightness of 340 cd/m2, viewing angles of 80° in the horizontal and vertical directions, and a refresh rate of 60 Hz. With its 16:9 aspect ratio, 1080p HD content is displayed without scaling or letterboxing. The display also features touch support, so you'll be able to utilize the multi-touch gestures incorporated into Windows 10 Pro. You can swipe, tap, and resize apps at your preference. The multi-touch display is protected by Corning Gorilla Glass 4 and an anti-smudge coating. The system is powered by a 1.6 GHz Intel Core i5- Quad-Core processor. It also has 8GB of 2133 MHz LPDDR3 RAM, a 256GB NVMe PCIe M.2 SSD, two USB 3.1 Gen 1 Type-C ports with support for DisplayPort, one USB 3.1 Gen 1 Type-A port with PowerShare, a built-in UHS-II microSD/SDHC/SDXC card reader, dual-band 802.11ac Wi-Fi (2x2), Bluetooth 4.1, dual integrated fixed-focus webcams (5MP front & 8MP rear), three integrated microphones (2 front & 1 rear), and stereo speakers that are driven by the Realtek ALC3253 codec with Waves MaxxAudio Pro. Included with this system are a 65W USB Type-C power adapter that operates using 100-240 VAC at 50-60 Hz and a 42Wh battery.
